Discover Marrakech

Begin your Moroccan adventure in the vibrant city of Marrakech. Explore the historic Medina, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and lose yourself in the colorful souks. Visit the iconic Koutoubia Mosque and the bustling Djemaa el-Fna square, where snake charmers, storytellers, and food vendors create an unforgettable atmosphere. End your day with a traditional welcome dinner at a local riad.

Day 2

Atlas Mountains & Berber Villages

Atlas Mountains & Berber Villages

Journey through the stunning Atlas Mountains, crossing the famous Tizi n'Tichka pass at 2,260m. Visit traditional Berber villages and learn about their ancient way of life. Stop at the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Benhaddou, a spectacular fortified village that has served as a backdrop for numerous Hollywood films. Continue to Ouarzazate, known as the 'Hollywood of Morocco'.

Day 3

Sahara Desert Adventure

Ouarzazate → Todra Gorge → Merzouga

Sahara Desert Adventure

Travel through the Valley of a Thousand Kasbahs to reach the impressive Todra Gorge. Marvel at the dramatic cliff walls rising up to 300m. Continue to Merzouga, gateway to the Sahara Desert. Mount your camel for a sunset trek through the golden dunes of Erg Chebbi. Spend the night in a luxury desert camp under the stars, enjoying traditional Berber music and a delicious dinner.

Frequently asked questions

What is a definite departure?

A definite departure means that the trip is guaranteed to run on the scheduled dates. Once a tour is marked as a definite departure, you can confidently book your flights and make other travel arrangements knowing that the tour will not be cancelled.

How can I book flights for my tour? What about extra hotel nights?

While we don't book flights directly, we can assist you with flight recommendations and timing. For the best rates and flexibility, we recommend booking your flights once the tour is confirmed as a definite departure. If you need extra hotel nights before or after the tour, we can arrange this for you at preferential rates. Just let us know your requirements when booking.

How much is the deposit for a tour?

To secure your booking, we require a deposit of 20% of the total tour price. This deposit is fully refundable if cancelled 60 days before departure. The remaining balance is due 30 days before the tour start date. For last-minute bookings made within 30 days of departure, full payment is required at the time of booking.
